FRED UI

FRED stands for "Friendly Robot to End Dementia". For this research project, I was tasked with creating a user interface (UI) for this friendly helper. It is constructed with a screen in its body that allows interaction. Our team has three designers and each of us are designing a different style of UI. I was selected to design a minimal/professional interface. The audience is a challenging one as dementia deteriorates a persons ability to do everyday tasks. Limiting selections and having courtesy navigation helps aid in use. I took an approach of looking at children's devices for my reference as they promote learning and do not feel overly simple. I created several user flows as this is closer to a device rather than an application. The logo was constructed to feel professional and trustworthy. The colors reference the idea that purple unites all dementia patients, and I build the palette around that.
